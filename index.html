The Opportunity

One-person companies are
the fastest-growing segment
in tech.

AI lets one person build, ship, and scale a product. But building alone doesn't mean building in isolation. What's missing isn't tools — it's community, direction, and a path forward.

01
Builders need direction
After shipping, most don't know what comes next — marketing, monetisation, scaling.
02
Knowledge is fragmented
Insights are scattered. Builders need it in one room, from real practitioners.
03
Community is the moat
The most valuable networks are built in rooms where people share real work and real feedback.
The Concept

A monthly series that
