The Concept · Division of Responsibilities
What We're Building.
Who Owns What.
A recurring monthly event — each episode follows a different phase of building and founding a one-person company. Designed for young solopreneurs and aspiring founders.
The Format
Two spaces. One evening.
Learn, demo, get real feedback.
🎓
Lobby — Learn + Demo
Opening speeches from Li Fan, Terence (Hashmeta), and Prof Liu Yang (NTU). Followed by SoulGarden and RiteSet demos, then 3 curated external app demos.
💬
New York Room — Show & Ask
Intimate feedback session. Builders present apps/ideas and get direct input from Li Fan, Wendy and Dannick on design. Application-based entry.
